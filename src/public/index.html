How it works

Three steps to a better study routine

No complicated setup. Fill in the details, get matched, and start studying.

1
Build a profile

Add the modules being studied, preferred study style, and weekly availability. Takes about two minutes.

2
Set preferences

Choose how to study: focused sessions, small groups, or open study rooms. Stubby filters matches accordingly.

3
Connect and study

Send a match request, chat directly, and schedule a session without leaving the platform.

Features

Everything needed to study smarter

Built around how students actually work, not how apps think they should.

Smart Matching

Matches are ranked by shared modules, schedule overlap, and study style, not just who signed up recently.

Session Scheduling

Book study sessions directly through Stubby. Set a time, location, and topic, and partners get notified instantly.

Built-in Chat

Message matched partners without switching apps. Share notes, links, or just coordinate where to meet.

Badges & Progress

Earn badges for consistent sessions and completed modules. A small nudge to keep the momentum going.

Match Preferences

Fine-tune who shows up in the feed. Filter by diploma, language, session type, and more.

Friend Network

Keep track of regular study partners. Build a network of people who are reliable and on the same track.
